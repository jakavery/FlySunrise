Why Sunrise?

Safety

With no major accidents since we were founded more than 30 years ago, no flight school around can match our safety record.

We own all of our own aircraft, which are maintained by our own expert aviation mechanics.

With safety at the forefront of everything we do, you know you're in good hands.

Efficiency

We have the largest fleet of training aircraft in the southeast and have plenty of expert instructors to ensure that you progress through your flight training at the pace you want. Multiple flights a day? Let's do it.

Quality

Our students have consistently high success rates on all exams. This is because we only hire the best instructors, and give you the individual, one-on-one attention you deserve.

Location

Flying in Florida means you get to enjoy more than 350 days of VFR flying weather each year.

When you're not at the airport, our proximity to beautiful beaches, Orlando theme parks, and other attractions means you'll always have something fun to do.

Stability

We've been training career pilots for over 30 years - not many of our competitors can say that.

That means we know what we're doing, and we'll always be around to meet your flying needs.
